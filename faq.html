fitness_center For Trainers

Click "Become a Trainer" and create your account. Fill out your profile with your qualifications, specialties, pricing, and availability. Once submitted, our team reviews your application (usually within 48 hours). You'll need to provide proof of certification.
We accept certifications from recognized bodies like ACE, NASM, ISSA, K11 Fitness, and other accredited institutions. Yoga teachers should have 200-hour or higher certification. If you're unsure about your certification, contact us and we'll review it.
Our Starter plan is completely free with limited features. Pro plan costs ₹999/month with full features. We also charge a small platform fee (10-15%) on each booking to cover payment processing and platform costs. See our pricing page for details.
After a session is marked as completed, payment is processed within 24-48 hours to your linked bank account. You can track all your earnings and pending payments in your trainer dashboard.
Complete your profile with a professional photo and detailed bio. Get verified for the Pro badge. Collect reviews from satisfied clients. Respond quickly to inquiries. Consider upgrading to Pro for priority search ranking and more visibility.
