Frequently Asked Questions

Is there really zero commission? expand_more
Yes. Most platforms take 20-30% of your earnings. At Onlifit, you keep 100% of the session fee. Clients pay a small service fee directly to us when booking, so you never see a cut in your paycheck.
When do I get paid? expand_more
Payouts are processed 24 hours after a successfully completed session directly to your linked bank account. No waiting for end-of-month cycles.
Can I train international clients? expand_more
Absolutely. If you offer online sessions, your profile can be discovered by clients globally. Our platform automatically handles timezone conversions and secure cross-border payments.
What happens if a client cancels? expand_more
You define your own cancellation policy during onboarding (e.g., 24-hour notice, strict no-refunds). We enforce it automatically to ensure your time is never wasted.
